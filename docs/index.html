Engineering Chronometric Interferometry

Driftlock Choir originated from precision oscillator research examining how sub-hertz frequency offsets accumulate across distributed nodes.

The result is chronometric interferometry: a control strategy that models oscillator ensembles as coupled systems, continuously estimating drift and applying nanosecond-scale phase corrections to maintain picosecond alignment.

"We turn oscillator drift into deterministic intelligence, enabling infrastructure to operate on a shared timebase regardless of scale or environment."
Hunter Bown, Founder
