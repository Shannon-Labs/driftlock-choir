# Slide 5 — Problem & Why Now

Title: Drift erodes distributed operations

Bullets
- Operators babysit fragmented tools while clocks, carriers, and data flows slip out of sync.
- Reliability losses compound into SLA penalties, manual rework, and mistrust across teams.
- AI autonomy surge amplifies the blast radius: more agents, more drift, less human oversight.
- Regulatory and safety scrutiny demand auditable synchronization before large-scale deployment.

Notes for presenter
- Ground the narrative in customer conversations (reliability, edge analytics, industrial autonomy).
- Quantify the pain with anecdotes: overnight pager storms, million-dollar downtime tickets, shadow spreadsheets.
- Set up the need for an evidence-backed, automation-friendly control layer.
