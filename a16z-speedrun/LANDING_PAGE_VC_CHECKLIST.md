# a16z Landing Page Checklist

## What VCs Look for in <7 Seconds

### ✅ The Hook (Above the Fold)
- [ ] **Instant understanding** - What do you do in 5 words or less?
- [ ] **Credibility signal** - Patent pending, validation metrics, or traction
- [ ] **The "Holy shit" number** - 22 picoseconds, 2,273× better than GPS
- [ ] **Natural curiosity** - "Wait, how is that possible?"

### ✅ The Pattern Recognition
VCs pattern match. Give them familiar frameworks:
- [ ] "The next Bell Labs moment" - They know Bell Labs = world-changing
- [ ] "3 radios → 47 → 1000s" - Natural network effects story
- [ ] "Deceptively simple" - The best innovations always are

### ✅ Social Proof Without Being Desperate
- [ ] Live deployment counter (even if small)
- [ ] Growing naturally (shows organic pull)
- [ ] Technical validation (Monte Carlo results)
- [ ] NO desperate "as seen in" logos

### ✅ The Landing Page Flow They Expect

1. **Hero**: Hook + one impossible metric
2. **Problem**: What's broken (GPS doesn't work indoors)
3. **Solution**: Your breakthrough (beats = time)
4. **How it works**: Simple enough for a partner meeting
5. **Traction**: Real numbers, even if small
6. **Vision**: Where this goes (mesh intelligence)
7. **Team**: Why you (but keep it brief)
8. **CTA**: Clear next step

## Current Landing Page Strengths ✅

- **Bell Labs framing**: Instantly positions as potentially world-changing
- **Deceptively simple**: Shows sophistication through simplicity
- **Natural growth story**: 3→47 radios feels organic
- **Technical depth available**: Can go deep if they want

## What Might Make Them Bounce ❌

- Too philosophical/abstract → Keep vision grounded in reality
- No clear business model → Add pricing tiers
- Too technical too fast → Lead with outcome, not process
- No urgency → Why now? (GPS vulnerabilities, 5G explosion)
